The challenge

Rosario's had been a fixture in Simi Valley for over 15 years, but their website was stuck in 2010. No mobile support, no online reservations, and buried on Google. Diners were finding competitors first.

What we built

We redesigned the entire site from scratch — a full-screen hero with appetizing photography, a clean digital menu organized by course, and an integrated reservation widget that feeds directly into their front-of-house system.

We also set up their Google Business Profile, submitted their sitemap, and implemented local SEO schema so they'd show up for "Italian restaurant near me" searches in Simi Valley and neighboring areas.

The result

Within 60 days of launch, online reservations tripled. They rank #1 in local search for their category. The owner told us it's the best investment they've made in years.
