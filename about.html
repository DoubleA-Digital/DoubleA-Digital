We started Double-A Digital because we got tired of seeing great local businesses get ignored online. So we decided to fix that.

Our story

Built in Simi Valley.
For Simi Valley.

Aarush and Ajay grew up in Simi Valley and watched the businesses that shaped this community struggle to compete online. Not because their products or services were bad — they weren't — but because they had outdated websites, no online presence, or were paying too much for too little.

So they teamed up, combined their skills in design and development, and built Double-A Digital with a simple promise: show you a real demo before you spend a dollar.

Every site is built by hand — no page builders, no outsourcing. When you work with Double-A Digital, you're working directly with the people who write the code.

What we stand for

Our values.

Local first

We're from here. We care about the same businesses you frequent — the coffee shop, the auto shop, the family restaurant. Local businesses deserve world-class websites.

No lock-in

You own everything — the code, the domain, the hosting account. We'll never hold your website hostage. If you want to take it somewhere else, it's yours to take.

We built a whole process around this. You should see exactly what you're getting before you commit. No surprises, no faith required. Just a real, working demo first.
